## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications ("Apps") published by the developer ("Developer," "we," "our," or "us") on the Apple App Store and any other platform where our Apps are distributed. By downloading or using any of our Apps, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

Each App may have different data practices. This policy is written to cover the full spectrum of our current and future Apps — from Apps that are entirely on-device and collect no data at all, to Apps that transmit data to cloud services or use Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Large Language Models (LLMs). Where an App's behavior differs from what is described here, the App itself and its App Store listing will clarify the specific practices that apply.

---

## 2. Data We May Collect

Depending on the App you use, we may collect some or all of the following categories of data. Some Apps collect none of this data.

### 2.1 Data You Provide Directly
- Account information (e.g., name, email address, username, password)
- User-generated content (e.g., text, images, documents, notes, audio, or other files you create or upload within the App)
- Communications you send to us (e.g., support requests, feedback)

### 2.2 Data Collected Automatically
- Device identifiers (e.g., device model, operating system version)
- App usage data and analytics (e.g., features accessed, session duration, crash reports)
- IP address and general location data (derived from network connection, unless a specific App requests precise location access with your permission)
- Diagnostic and performance data

### 2.3 Data Processed by AI / LLM Features
Some Apps include features powered by Artificial Intelligence, including Large Language Models (LLMs). In those Apps:

- Content you submit to an AI feature (e.g., prompts, text, images) may be processed by on-device models or transmitted to cloud-based AI services, depending on the App.
- Where AI processing occurs entirely on-device, your data never leaves your device for that purpose.
- Where AI processing occurs in the cloud, your content is transmitted to a third-party AI provider (see Section 5) solely to generate a response for you.

### 2.4 No Data Collected (Local-Only Apps)
Some of our Apps are designed to operate entirely on your device. If an App's App Store listing states that no data is collected, the App processes all information locally and does not transmit any personal data to us or any third party.

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use data only for the following purposes:

- **App Functionality:** To operate, maintain, and improve the features of the App.
- **AI Feature Delivery:** To process your inputs and return AI-generated responses, where applicable.
- **Account Management:** To create and manage your account, if the App includes accounts.
- **Security and Fraud Prevention:** To detect, investigate, and prevent unauthorized access or abuse.
- **Analytics and Improvement:** To understand how our Apps are used, fix bugs, and develop new features. Where possible, this data is anonymized or aggregated.
- **Customer Support:** To respond to your questions or resolve issues you report.
- **Legal Compliance:** To meet our obligations under applicable laws and regulations.

We do **not** sell your personal data. We do **not** use your data for advertising or tracking across third-party apps and websites.

---

## 4. On-Device vs. Cloud Processing

We design our Apps with a privacy-first approach. Data processing falls into one of the following modes, which is disclosed in each App's description:

| Mode | Description |
|---|---|
| **Fully Local** | All data stays on your device. No data is sent to us or any third party. |
| **Cloud-Assisted** | Some data (e.g., AI prompts) is sent to cloud services to provide functionality. This is disclosed in the App. |
| **Hybrid** | Certain features are on-device; others use cloud services. Disclosure is provided per feature. |

## 5. Third-Party Services

Some Apps integrate with third-party services to provide functionality, including but not limited to:

- **AI and LLM Providers** (e.g., Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, Apple Intelligence, or similar providers) — used to power AI features where cloud processing is required.
- **Cloud Storage Providers** — used to sync or back up your data if the App offers that feature.
- **Analytics Providers** — used to collect anonymized diagnostic and usage information to improve App stability and performance.
- **Authentication Providers** — used to enable secure sign-in (e.g., Sign in with Apple).
- **Payment Processors** — used to process in-app purchases (handled by Apple's App Store infrastructure).

Any third-party service we use is contractually required to handle your data securely, consistent with this policy and applicable law. We do not authorize third parties to use your data for their own marketing or advertising purposes.

The specific third-party services used by a given App may be detailed in that App's App Store Privacy Nutrition Label and supplementary disclosures within the App.

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

- **Service Providers:** With third parties acting on our behalf as described in Section 5, solely to deliver the App's functionality.
- **Legal Requirements:** If required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
- **Business Transfers:** In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred. We will notify you via a prominent notice in the App or this policy before such a transfer takes effect.
- **With Your Consent:** For any other purpose, only with your explicit prior consent.

---

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. Specifically:

- **On-device data** is retained on your device until you delete the App or use in-App deletion features.
- **Cloud-stored data** is retained for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 9).
- **AI prompt data** sent to cloud providers for processing is subject to the data retention policies of those providers. We will disclose the relevant provider in the App so you can review their policies.
- **Analytics data** is retained in anonymized or aggregated form and does not retain personal identifiers beyond the period needed for analysis.

---

## 8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

- Encryption of data in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
- Encryption of data at rest where applicable
- Access controls limiting who can access your data
- Regular security reviews

No system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

## 9. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Access:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correction:** Request that we correct inaccurate data.
- **Deletion:** Request that we delete your data. Where an App allows account creation, you can initiate account and data deletion from within the App, or by contacting us (see Section 13).
- **Portability:** Request your data in a portable format.
- **Restriction / Objection:** Request that we restrict or stop certain processing of your data.
- **Withdraw Consent:** Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in Section 13. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

## 10. Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

## 11. International Users

Our Apps are available globally. If you are located outside the country where our servers or service providers are based, your data may be transferred internationally. Where such transfers occur, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place (such as standard contractual clauses or equivalent mechanisms) in accordance with applicable law, including the GDPR for users in the European Economic Area.

### EU / EEA Users (GDPR)
Our legal bases for processing your data include: performance of a contract, legitimate interests, compliance with legal obligations, and — where required — your explicit consent. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

### California Users (CCPA / CPRA)
We do not sell or share your personal information as defined under California law. California residents may exercise the rights described in Section 9 by contacting us.
