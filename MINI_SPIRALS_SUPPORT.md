# Mini Spirals — Support

Mini Spirals is a wrist-first tracker for the small, repeating choices that shape a day.

## How it works
- **Up / down** — tap whether the moment just made your life better or worse.
- **Edge slider** — set where you are now on a red-to-green scale.
- **Break the loop** — tap to mitigate a repeating pattern; the day resets.
- **Focus timer** — a five-minute boredom trainer; tap Redirect when your attention wanders.
- **Trends** — cycles Today, your cumulative life line, and the edge slider.
- **Daily goal** — set how many ups you aim for each day.
